Alfredo Pasta

Home

Description

Alfredo pasta is a creamy Italian inspired dish made with butter, Parmesan cheese, chicken or beef and pasta. It is rich, comforting, and quick to prepare, making it a popular choice for weeknight dinners.

Ingredients(to serve 2 people)

  • 200g fettuccine
  • Butter or oil
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese or cream cheese
  • Grated Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a bowl of boiling water and salt.
  2. Prepare the sauce.
  3. Combine the pasta and sauce.
  4. Serve.
